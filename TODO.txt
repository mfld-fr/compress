The SE and SI algorithms optimize encoding cost in different manners.

The SE iterates on the kept symbols, drops the symbol with less gain,
with reference bits adjusted to the kept symbols.

The SI iterates on the reference bits, with kept symbols adjusted to that
reference bits. It is faster than SE.

Apply the faster SI cost optimization to SE.
