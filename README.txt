
This compressor targets the embedded devices with limited resources:
- CPU: 8 or 16 bits
- memory: < 64 KB

Inspired by the famous & venerable Exomizer for 6502 targets:
https://github.com/bitshifters/exomizer

Current status:
- internal loopback test OK
- good ratio compared to GZip
